Deep Blue Grill

Use

The Deep Blue Grill is an essential block used to cook and process all of the items added by Blue Depths. Without it, you cannot cook tuna fillets into tuna steaks, process nematocyst gel into slimeballs, etc. It can also function as a normal smoker to cook food from vanilla Minecraft.

The way to cook food specifically added by Blue Depths is stricter than with most other food sources in Minecraft. In most cases, you can only cook the bass as 1, 8, 16, or 64 using either sticks (only if you want to cook one item), coal, or charcoal as fuel. This mostly applies for obtaining the following food sources:

  • Cooked Largemouth Bass
  • Cooked Catfish
  • Tuna Steak

For obtaining other items through the Deep Blue Grill, you can look at the following links:

  • Nematocyst Gel → Slimeballs
  • Sturgeon Scute → Armorbite
