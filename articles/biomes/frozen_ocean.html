Spawning

Blue Depths adds one new mob to Frozen and Deep Frozen Oceans, the orca. The way these orcas spawn is by randomly replacing a specific vanilla mob that naturally spawn. For the case of frozen and deep frozen oceans, the salmon is the mob that has a chance to be replaced by the new Blue Depths mobs. Ecosystem Details

Predatory/Hostile Mobs

The creature added to frozen oceans, who in most cases are friendly, but nonetheless is still a predator is the Orca. These cetaceans act exactly the same as regular dolphins, except when provoke like regular dolphins, will deal way more damage than regular dolphins. It is best to stay nice to these creatures, otherwise it's partner and other surrounding orcas will start attacking you.
