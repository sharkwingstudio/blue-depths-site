Mangrove Swamps

Basic Info

Risk Level: Safe

Aquatic Mobs

  • Tropical Fish (Very Common)
  • Warm Frog (Common)
  • Catfish (Common)

Spawning

The aquatic mobs that spawn in Mangrove Swamps include tropical fish, warm frogs, and catfish. Of these mobs, the catfish is the only new mob that spawns with Blue Depths. For each tropical fish, there is a chance that it can be replaced by a catfish. The table below shows the spawn rates of Blue Depths-exclusive mobs in mangrove swamps.

Spawn Chance (Tropical Fish)
